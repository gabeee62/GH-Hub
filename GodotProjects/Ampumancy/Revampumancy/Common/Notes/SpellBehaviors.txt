Different ways spells are cast:
	- Charge and release
	- Instant cast
	- Persistent cast

Different things spells can do:
	- Summon a projectile
	- Move the player/enemies
	- Apply effects to the player/enemies
	- Summon familiars
	- Move world objects
	- Apply effects to certain world objects

Different ways spells must be prepared to use:
	- Supplying the corresponding mana and materials yourself
	- Crafting a catalyst to cover materials
